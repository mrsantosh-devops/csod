1. Get application URL by running these commands:

  kubectl get svc {{ include "nodejs-app.fullname" . }}

2. To upgrade chart:
  helm upgrade RELEASE_NAME ./nodejs-chart -f values.yaml

3. To rollback a release to previous revision:
  helm rollback RELEASE_NAME REVISION

4. You can also use kubectl rollout:
  kubectl rollout status deployment/{{ include "nodejs-app.fullname" . }}
  kubectl rollout undo deployment/{{ include "nodejs-app.fullname" . }}
